Banging Noodles

A Brief Tale

As with everyone else, instant noodles have been a staple throughout my life in university.

This is my way of perking them up a bit for those times where you're craving something more.

Ingredients

  • any pack of instant noodles (i like shin black)
  • 2-3 eggs
  • chinese chili oil

Steps

  1. Cook the instant noodles as per the instructions on the packet, cook in a wide skillet.
  2. Once noodles have softened, add eggs and cover.
  3. Once eggs have cooked to your desired doneness, add salt, pepper, and chili oil.
  4. Enjoy straight from the pan.
