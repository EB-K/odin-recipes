EB's Pasta

A Brief Tale

This spectacular pasta is the result of my brilliant ability of inputing minimum cooking effort and yielding maximum taste.

This dish tastes great wether you follow the recipe to the tee or play by your own rules, as long as you stick to the fundementals you'll be golden.

Eyeball the measurements, thats how I roll.

Ingredients

  • pasta of choice (i prefer squiggly macaroni *see picture*)
  • ground beef (or any ground meat you have)
  • various peppers (i like a mix of capsicum and jalepeno)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • tumeric
  • garlic
  • tomato paste
  • chili powder
  • parmesan cheese

Optional Ingredients

  • butter
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • heavy cream
  • paprika
  • chili flakes

Steps

  1. Bring your water to a boil and add your pasta, don't forget to salt the water.
  2. While the water is boiling, dice your vegetables and add some oil(if you have) to a large pan.
  3. Add spices to the meat along with tomato paste and veg.
  4. Allow meat to cook and remove pasta from heat once al dente.
  5. If you have cream and butter, now would be the time to add it to the meat. Allow the sauce to reduce until at your desired thickness.
  6. Add pasta to cooked meat along with a bit of the pasta water.
  7. Thoroughly mix the pasta and top with parmesan cheese.
  8. Dish is best served hot.
